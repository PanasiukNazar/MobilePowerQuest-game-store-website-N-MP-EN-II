Privacy Policy

Welcome to our Privacy Policy. Protecting your personal information is a top priority for our PC and console games online store.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how our gaming store collects, stores, and uses personal information ("Data") when you browse our site, purchase games, or contact support. It also outlines your rights regarding your information.

2. Collection of Personal Information

We only collect personal data you provide voluntarily, such as when registering an account, placing orders for PC or console games, or submitting support requests. Certain site features may not be available if you choose not to provide required information.

3. Collection of Non-Personal Information

When visiting our website, we automatically gather technical details like your IP address, browser type, visited pages, and duration of visits. This data is used exclusively to improve our gaming platform and analyze user behavior anonymously.

4. Use of Collected Data

Your data is utilized solely to process your orders, deliver digital content, inform you about new game releases, or respond to inquiries. Non-personal data is used to enhance technical performance and user experience on our platform.

5. Cookies

Cookies help us enhance your shopping experience by saving preferences like language settings and cart contents. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit certain store functionalities.

6. Advertising and Tracking

Our store uses advertising tools such as Google Ads and Facebook Pixel to offer tailored game recommendations based on your browsing and purchase history. You can manage or opt out of these cookies here: https://optout.networkadvertising.org/

7. Data Security

We implement advanced technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Only authorized staff members of our gaming store have access to your data.

8. External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party gaming studios or partner sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before sharing any personal information.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to comply with legal requirements or improve our processes. We recommend checking this page regularly for any changes.
