Cookie Policy – Online Store for PC and Console Games

At our online store for PC and console games, we prioritize your privacy and security. To enhance your shopping experience, we use cookies. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, the types we employ, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files saved on your device (such as your computer, smartphone, or console) when you visit our website. These files store information that helps remember your preferences and actions over time. Cookies improve site functionality and allow us to personalize your experience.

Different cookies serve different purposes. Some are essential for the site’s operation, while others help us analyze user behavior or display targeted promotions for games and gaming gear.

2. Why Do We Use Cookies?

We use cookies for the following reasons:

  • Enhanced User Experience: Your preferences like favorite gaming platforms (PC, PlayStation, Xbox), genres, or wishlists are saved to avoid repetitive input.
  • Personalization: We provide tailored game recommendations and special offers based on your browsing history.
  • Performance & Analytics: Cookies help us understand how users interact with our store to improve content and navigation.
  • Advertising: Cookies enable us to promote games, console bundles, or pre-orders that match your interests.
  • Security: Certain cookies ensure secure login and protect your data during checkout.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Our platform utilizes the following cookie categories:

3.1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly, such as keeping your selected games in the shopping cart.

  • Session Cookies: Retain your platform or genre choices during your visit.
  • Security Cookies: Protect your login credentials and payment details.

3.2. Analytics Cookies

These collect anonymous data on popular games and pages, helping us optimize our store.

  • Google Analytics: Tracks user engagement with game listings and store sections.

3.3. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember your language preferences, platform selections, and filters to enhance your shopping.

  • Language Settings: Saves your preferred store language.
  • Filter Preferences: Keeps your search filters (platform, price range, genre) for future visits.

3.4. Advertising Cookies

These cookies display personalized ads for games, consoles, and accessories aligned with your profile.

  • Google Ads: Shows promotions based on your browsing habits.
  • Facebook Pixel: Supports targeted marketing for new game releases on social platforms.

3.5. Social Media Cookies

These allow you to share content like new releases on networks such as Facebook and Twitter, and help us track engagement.

  • Facebook: Facilitates sharing favorite games and deals.
  • Twitter: Enables quick sharing of gaming news and titles.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control or block cookies anytime through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our store.

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Site Settings > Cookies
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Privacy, Search, and Services > Cookies and Site Permissions

5. External Links

Our website may link to other gaming platforms or publishers. This cookie policy applies solely to our online store. We recommend reviewing privacy policies of external sites separately.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this cookie policy as needed. All changes will be posted here. Please check back regularly for updates.
